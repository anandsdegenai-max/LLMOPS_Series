Agentic AI is a type of AI system that can act autonomously to achieve goals, not just respond to prompts. It can plan, decide, use tools, and execute multiple steps to complete a task with minimal human input.

In simple terms:

Traditional AI: responds to a question
Agentic AI: figures out how to solve a problem and performs actions to achieve the goal.

Key Idea

Agentic AI systems behave like digital agents that can:

Understand a goal

Plan steps to achieve it

Use tools or APIs

Execute tasks

Evaluate results and adjust

Example
Traditional LLM (like a normal chatbot)

User:

"Find the best laptop under $1500."

The model just generates text suggestions.

Agentic AI

User:

"Find the best laptop under $1500 and email me the top 3 deals."

Agentic AI will:

Search the internet

Compare prices

Analyze reviews

Pick top options

Send an email with results
